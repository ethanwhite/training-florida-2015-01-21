General Information

Software Carpentry and Data Carpentry's mission is to help scientists and engineers get more research done in less time and with less pain by teaching them basic lab skills for scientific computing. This hands-on two-day workshop will introduce people to our teaching methods and curriculum.

Who: This course is designed for people who are already familiar with some of the basic content of either Software Carpentry or Data Carpentry and wish to teach workshops. Note that participants do not need to be experts in the topics these workshops cover, but should have taken part in a workshop before attending.
